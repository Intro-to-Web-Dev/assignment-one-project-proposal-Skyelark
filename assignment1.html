Comfort soup recipes to nourish the body and soul

Goals

  1. Curated collection of tasty soups.
  2. Source of comfort when feeling under the weather.
  3. Complete instructions with pictures.

Inspiration

I want to model my website after Simply Recipes, specifically their Sandwich Recipes page. I think this style would be a great fit for my website because it is a good format to showcase a handful of recipes and easy to navigate. You can click on the pictures and be taken to the individual recipe page where you can find all the information on that particular item. The layout is intuitive and minimalistic, but it should fit my needs.

Target Audience

The target audience for my site is adults looking to cook at home. They may be cooking for a family. They are looking for something homemade and gormet, something that will be worth the effort to make. They are looking for classic soups with no frills but lots of flavor.
